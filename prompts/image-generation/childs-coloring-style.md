## 中文说明

适用于细节 1 和 2 的具体细节，以及第三个细节的语义细节。就像我使用的示例图像一样：
用蜡笔涂上[细节1]、[细节2]和[细节3]的卡通[设置]场景，就像一个学习的孩子。
设置=“日落”
详细信息1=“树木”
Detail2=“灌木丛”
Detail3="流经森林的小溪"

## 中文 Prompt 正文

```md
卡通${setting}场景，蜡笔颜色为${detail1}、${detail2}和${detail3}，就像一个学习的孩子。
```

## English Original

### Title

Childs Coloring Style

### Description

Works well with specific details for Detail 1 and 2, and then a semantic detail for the third one. Like in the example image i used:
A cartoon [SETTING] scene with crayon colored [Detail1] and [Detail2] and [Detail3], like that of a learning child.
Setting="Sunset"
Detail1="trees"
Detail2="bushes"
Detail3="creek flowing through forest"

### Prompt

```md
A cartoon ${setting} scene with crayon colored ${detail1} and ${detail2} and ${detail3}, like that of a learning child.
```
