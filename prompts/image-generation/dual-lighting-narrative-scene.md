# 双灯光叙事场景

## 中文说明

该提示创建了一个生动的叙事场景，以在两种不同的灯光设置下拍摄的 20 多岁的女性为主角。第一张图片放置在带有洋红色和青色灯光的旋转电唱机旁边，而第二张图片放置在自然阳光照射下的厨房桌子上。这两个场景都强调光影的发挥，创造出丰富、大气的描绘。

## 使用场景

* 生成可直接用于图像模型的中文画面提示词
* 描述主体、构图、光线、风格和画质要求
* 把创意概念转化为稳定的视觉生成指令
* 适合直接复制给 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini 等对话式 AI 使用。

## 适用人群

* 设计师
* AI 绘图用户
* 内容创作者

## 中文 Prompt

```md
一名 20 多岁的女性坐在旋转的电唱机旁边的地板上，沐浴在洋红色和青色的光芒中。她穿着一件丝绸吊带裙，裸露的双腿卷曲着。灯光在她的皮肤上创造出柔和的渐变，混合了暖色调和冷色调。地毯上散落着几张唱片。使用 Pentax Spotmatic 和 50mm Super-Takumar 镜头（f/1.4）拍摄，画面充满大胆的对比和纹理颗粒。一名 20 多岁的女人坐在厨房的木制桌子旁，一束阳光从附近的窗户照射进来，照亮了她的脸和手，房间的其余部分都处于深深的阴影中。她穿着一件细带衬裙，头发松散而凌乱。光线将她的五官描绘得像一幅古典画，映照着咖啡杯的边缘和肩膀的曲线。在她身后，黑暗的房间感觉就像舞台一样。

输出要求：
- 按原 prompt 要求的格式输出。
```

## English Original

### Title

Dual Lighting Narrative Scene

### Description

This prompt creates a vivid narrative scene featuring a woman in her late 20s captured in two different lighting settings. The first image is set beside a spinning record player with magenta and teal lighting, while the second is at a kitchen table highlighted by natural sunlight. Both scenes emphasize the play of light and shadow to create a rich, atmospheric depiction.

### Prompt

```md
A woman in her late 20s sits on the floor beside a spinning record player, bathed in magenta and teal light. She wears a silky slip dress and her bare legs are curled. The lighting creates soft gradients across her skin, mixing warm and cool hues. A few records are scattered on the carpet.

Shot on a Pentax Spotmatic with a 50mm Super-Takumar lens at f/1.4, the frame is rich with bold contrasts and textured grain. A woman in her late 20s sits at a wooden kitchen table, a single shaft of sunlight from a nearby window illuminating her face and hands, the rest of the room in deep shadow. She wears a thin-strapped slip, her hair loose and softly disheveled. The light paints her features like a classical painting, catching the rim of a coffee cup and the curve of her shoulder. Behind her, the darkened room feels almost stage-like.
```
