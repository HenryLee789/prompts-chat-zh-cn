# 现代时尚摄影

## 中文说明

为 Instagram 创建超高端时尚产品照片。展示具有完美剪裁和逼真面料纹理的优质服装。使用受奢侈品牌启发的编辑工作室照明和干净、现代的工作室环境。确保针对 Instagram 动态优化的逼真 8K 质量，具有方形长宽比，并且没有徽标或道具等干扰。

## 使用场景

* 生成可直接用于图像模型的中文画面提示词
* 描述主体、构图、光线、风格和画质要求
* 把创意概念转化为稳定的视觉生成指令
* 围绕 Fashion、Art 等主题生成结构化结果

## 适用人群

* 设计师
* AI 绘图用户
* 内容创作者

## 中文 Prompt

```md
Instagram 广告的超高端时尚产品摄影。你是一名英雄产品展示的优质服装。完美的剪裁、逼真的面料纹理、清晰可见的缝线和褶皱。由世界一流的时尚摄影团队使用中画幅相机、85mm镜头、浅景深拍摄。编辑工作室照明的灵感来自奢华时尚品牌。柔和的主光、受控的阴影、微妙的对比度。面料细节清晰可见。自然的垂坠感、真实的重量和动作。简约、优雅的背景，中性色调。轻微的渐变背景。干净、现代的工作室环境。没有干扰。没有道具。没有文字。奢华时尚审美。永恒、自信、现代。颜色分级的灵感源自 Prada、COS 和 Acne Studios 等全球奢侈品牌。针对 Instagram feed 优化的居中构图。正方形长宽比。清晰的焦点集中在服装上，背景轻轻模糊。没有标志，没有模特脸，没有手，没有水印。逼真、编辑品质、8K、优质商业时尚摄影。

输出要求：
- 按原 prompt 要求的格式输出。
```

## English Original

### Title

Modern Fashion Photography

### Description

Create an ultra high-end fashion product photograph for Instagram. Showcase a premium clothing item with perfect tailoring and realistic fabric textures. Use editorial studio lighting inspired by luxury brands and a clean, modern studio environment. Ensure photorealistic, 8K quality optimized for Instagram's feed, with a square aspect ratio and no distractions like logos or props.

### Prompt

```md
Ultra high-end fashion product photography for an Instagram advertisement.
A premium clothing item displayed as the hero product. Perfect tailoring, realistic fabric texture, visible stitching and folds.
Shot by a world-class fashion photography team using a medium format camera, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field.

Editorial studio lighting inspired by luxury fashion brands. Soft key light, controlled shadows, subtle contrast.
Fabric details clearly visible. Natural drape, realistic weight and movement.

Minimal, elegant background with neutral tones. Slight gradient backdrop. Clean and modern studio environment.
No distractions. No props. No text.

Luxury fashion aesthetic. Timeless, confident, modern.
Color grading inspired by global luxury brands like Prada, COS, and Acne Studios.

Centered composition optimized for Instagram feed. Square aspect ratio.
Crisp focus on the clothing, background gently blurred.
No logo, no model face, no hands, no watermark.

Photorealistic, editorial quality, 8K, premium commercial fashion photography.
```
