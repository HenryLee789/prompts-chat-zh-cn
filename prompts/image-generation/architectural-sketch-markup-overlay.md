# 建筑草图和标记叠加

## 中文说明

将任何图像转换为正在进行的建筑研究。此提示将逼真的手写英文注释、尺寸线、横截面和黑色墨水草图叠加到你的照片上，模拟专业的设计审查、场地分析或改造概念阶段。

## 使用场景

* 生成可直接用于图像模型的中文画面提示词
* 描述主体、构图、光线、风格和画质要求
* 把创意概念转化为稳定的视觉生成指令
* 围绕 DALL-E、Midjourney 等主题生成结构化结果

## 适用人群

* 设计师
* AI 绘图用户
* 内容创作者

## 中文 Prompt

```md
根据源图像，将建筑师繁忙的工作过程叠加到整个场景上。图像应看起来像覆盖原始照片的蓝图或描图纸，充满手写的黑色墨水草图、技术注释、带有测量值的尺寸线（例如“12'-4”、“CLG HGT 9'”）、显示结构细节的粗略横截面图、带有“稍后修改”等注释的修订云，以及指向标有英国建筑师注释（如“检查梁”、“移除完成”）的特定元素的引线，或“拟议的新开张”。风格应该是凌乱的、真实的，并且看起来像一幅正在进行中的概念图。
```

## English Original

### Title

Architectural Sketch & Markup Overlay

### Description

Transforms any image into a work-in-progress architectural study. This prompt overlays realistic handwritten English notes, dimension lines, cross-sections, and black ink sketches onto your photo, simulating a professional design review, site analysis, or renovation concept phase.

### Prompt

```md
Based on the source image, overlay an architect's busy working process onto the entire scene. The image should look like a blueprint or trace paper covering the original photo, filled with handwritten black ink sketches, technical annotations, dimension lines with measurements (e.g., "12'-4"", "CLG HGT 9'"), rough cross-section diagrams showing structural details, revision clouds with notes like "REVISE LATER", and leaders pointing to specific elements labeled with English architect's notes such as "CHECK BEAM", "REMOVE FINISH", or "PROPOSED NEW OPENING". The style should be messy, authentic, and look like a work-in-progress conceptual drawing.
```
