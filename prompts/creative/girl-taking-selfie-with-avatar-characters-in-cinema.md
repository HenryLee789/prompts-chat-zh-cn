Girl Taking Selfie with Avatar Characters in Cinema

### Description

Create a realistic 8k resolution image of a girl taking a selfie with Avatar movie characters in a cinema hall. The girl wears a black t-shirt with 'AVATAR' and blue jeans. The background includes cinema seats and a large screen.
